What I hope to practice/learn?

How might I build this?

  1. Logo <img> with <p> tags for the text
  2. <ul> for the nav
  3. <section> for each slide
  4. <article> for the slide content
  5. <h2> and <p> for the header and paragraph in the slide
  6. <button> or <a> for button in slide
  7. <ul> for the slide buttons
